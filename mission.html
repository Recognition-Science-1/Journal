Essential Vocabulary

Understanding Recognition Science requires familiarity with key concepts that form the foundation of our theory. These terms represent precise mathematical and physical concepts, not philosophical abstractions.

Recognition Event
A discrete, indivisible interaction where one pattern "recognizes" another, creating both an energy cost and credit that must balance. The fundamental unit of reality.
Recognition Ledger
The universal accounting system that tracks all recognition events. Every particle, force, and phenomenon is an entry in this cosmic ledger that must maintain perfect balance.

The mathematical constant φ = (1+√5)/2 ≈ 1.618034. Proven to be the only scaling factor that preserves recognition balance, making it fundamental to all physical laws.
Rung
A position in the cosmic hierarchy where particles exist. Each rung r corresponds to an energy E = E_coh × φ^r, allowing precise mass predictions for all particles.
Coherence Energy (E_coh)
The fundamental energy scale E_coh = 0.09473154 eV, derived from the Higgs mass. All particle masses are multiples of this energy scaled by powers of φ.
Eight-Beat Closure
The principle that all stable patterns complete in cycles of eight recognition events. This generates the gauge symmetries of the Standard Model.
Consciousness Threshold
The complexity level at rung 67 where self-recognition becomes possible. When pattern complexity reaches φ^67 ≈ 10^14, consciousness emerges.
Dual-Recognition Balance
The fundamental principle that every recognition event creates equal and opposite cost and credit, ensuring the universe maintains perfect ledger balance at all scales.
Self-Similarity
The property that recognition patterns repeat across all scales with golden ratio proportions, creating the fractal structure of reality from quantum to cosmic scales.
Machine-Verifiable
All claims in Recognition Science are formally proven in Lean 4 theorem prover, ensuring mathematical rigor and eliminating human error or bias from our proofs.
Zero Free Parameters
Unlike other theories, Recognition Science contains no adjustable constants. Every physical quantity emerges mathematically from the eight foundational axioms.

# Mission & Foundational Vocabulary

## 🎯 Our Mission

**To maintain a public, machine-verifiable ledger in which every observable phenomenon is traced—without adjustable constants—to a finite graph of bidirectional recognition axioms.**

This ledger is not an archive; it is the **kernel of an operating system** that humans, AIs, and future minds will extend, debug, or fork only by adding or removing axioms under transparent audit.

---

## 📚 Foundational Vocabulary

### **Recognition Link**
The minimal bidirectional act: two events predicting each other with the least possible information overhead.

*Example*: When an electron recognizes a photon, both events become mutually predictive with minimal information exchange.

### **Axiom Graph** 
A finite, acyclic network of recognition links taken as primitive truth.

*Current Status*: Our foundation rests on 8 core axioms forming a complete, consistent graph.

### **Proof Object**
A machine-checkable chain of entailments from axiom nodes to a theorem node, stored with an immutable hash.

*Technical Note*: All proof objects are formalized in Lean 4 and cryptographically signed.

### **Prediction Hash**
A digest that binds a proof object to a quantitative, testable forecast.

*Example*: `sha256(proof_electron_mass) → 0.51099895_MeV ± 0.00000015_MeV`

### **Truth Packet**
The bundle (proof object, {prediction hashes}) whose live status—pending, verified, refuted—is updated automatically as data arrive.

*Lifecycle*: Submission → Validation → Monitoring → Resolution → Canonisation

## 🎯 Core Principles

### **P1: Axiomatic Completion**
Claims must be expressible—or mappable without free parameters—to the recognition grammar.

**Why This Matters**: Eliminates the proliferation of adjustable constants that plague traditional physics.

### **P2: Machine-Auditable Proofs** 
Proofs ship in a formal language an open-source verifier can check end-to-end.

**Implementation**: All proofs must compile in Lean 4 without `sorry` statements.

### **P3: Push-Button Reproducibility**
Data and code containers must execute headlessly; manual steps are disallowed.

**Standard**: One-click reproduction of all numerical results with fixed random seeds.

### **P4: Bidirectional Learning**
Scientific credit is proportional to prediction hashes that survive contact with reality.

**Innovation**: Negative results earn equal prestige to positive confirmations.

### **P5: Negative Elevation**
Refutations and tighter bounds earn the same prestige as positive confirmations; reducing uncertainty advances the kernel.

**Impact**: Transforms science from confirmation bias to uncertainty reduction.
